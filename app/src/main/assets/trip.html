The Trip screen allows you to QUICKLY book hotels at cheapest rates offered by Expedia.
To Book A Hotel Room
  • Make sure you are connected to Internet.
  • Set a destination (from Map, Find, or Plan screen) where you would like to book the hotel.
  • Cities, and localities can be entered in Find screen as address,Manhattan
  • Press the "Hotels" button on this screen, then select the criteria for the hotel room.
  • Press the "Find" button on the criteria box to get a list of hotels with prices, pictures, and ratings etc.
  • Touch a top of a column on the list of hotels to sort based on that column.
  • Click on the price of a particular hotel to open the booking page in the browser.
  • Expedia booking will take over from here on.
  • Note: All customer support will be provided by Expedia.
  • Note: Your search preferences will be saved for the next time when you search a room.
